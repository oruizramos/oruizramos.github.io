Deleo

  • Play the game: Here

Notable technologies/tools used


  • Game Maker for coding/engine
  • Aseprite for pixel art

Context of the project


I developed this project under the instructions of creating a game with a political theme. I chose imperialism.

Description


Deleo is a short and simple 2D platforming game with real time strategy elements, which portraits the means of imperialism (and the resultant consequences of it such as war and resource hogging/appropriation). The objective is to capture/control the majority of territory and reserves of the land, while taking the others' as well, in order to monopolize everything.

Important game design choices

-I picked such name from the latin "dēleō", which means to delete, raze, annilate and terminate.

-I decided to make an analogy between the game elements and ramping difficulty/progression of each of the five stages, to the "phases" of imperialism: it starts slows and subtle(just securing/taking resources), but ultimately, it becomes predatory and violent(use of repelling tools -magnet- and weapons -gatling gun-).

-The choice of using robots and tanks is to depict the usual militarization of such activity.

-The infamous' war text quotes/phrases used in between each level serve to create uneasiness in the player. They are there NOT to support such ideologies, but to denounce and condemn them.
