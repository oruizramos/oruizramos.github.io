About me



Hi! I am Omar Ramos. I am a multidisciplinary/versatile UI/UX, Game, Web and Narrative Designer, originally from Mexico, currently based in Cologne, Germany.

I have always been passionate about the potential that good visual and narrative discourse can create in all media and art forms, particularly, in videogames.
